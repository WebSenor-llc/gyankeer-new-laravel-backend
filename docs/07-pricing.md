## Frequently asked pricing questions

### Is there a per-employee charge?

Pricing is per **employee bracket**, not strict per-employee. So 80
employees on Starter is the same as 50 employees on Starter — the bracket
caps at 100. If you cross 100, upgrade to Professional.

### What if I have multiple companies?

| | Plan limit | Add-on |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | 1 company | — (upgrade to Professional) |
| Professional | 5 companies | ₹500/mo per extra company |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | — |

Companies share employees data only with explicit permission (data isolation
is the default).

### Can I move from On-premise to Cloud later?

Yes. We migrate your data and you get the cloud subscription. Migration is
free for Enterprise customers, ₹15,000 for Professional.

### What if my Govt rate changes mid-year?

For example, when Maharashtra increased PT slab in 2024 — you don't wait
for a software upgrade. Open `/settings`, edit the slab, save. Effective
immediately for the next payroll run.

### Can I get a free trial?

30 days, full Professional plan, no credit card required. Contact us with
your company details and we set up a sandboxed instance with sample data.

### Is there an academic / NGO discount?

50% off for registered NGOs and educational institutions. Contact for
documentation requirements.

### Refund policy?

Monthly plans: cancel anytime, refund pro-rated.
Annual plans: cancel within 30 days for 100% refund; after 30 days, no
refund but you keep access until expiry.
